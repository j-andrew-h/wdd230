Fitt's Law

BrowserStack

browserstack.com

The law of Fitt's Law states that the time required to click an element on the website should be the minimun possible, the website makes use of large buttons close to the middle of the page on the left side for the most important buttons necessary.

Proximity

Wall Street Journal

wsj.com

The principle of proximity is shown through the grouping of the elements that show the articles being shown, with a title, author, and brief description being shown close together for each article.

Alignment

DnD 5e WikiDot
